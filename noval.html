🧠 Why This Story Matters to Me

_Thirty Things Map We Made_ isn’t just a story — it’s a piece of my heart. It was born out of late-night thoughts, teenage dreams, and the pressure of figuring out who we are before anyone else tells us who to be. This book follows three close friends — Ava, Milan, and Zara — as they take a break from exams and expectations to explore the world and, more importantly, themselves. Through 30 experiences, they chase freedom, friendship, and clarity. I wrote this because I believe we all deserve a pause — to breathe, to feel lost, and to find our own map again.
