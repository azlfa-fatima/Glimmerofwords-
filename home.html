MEET THE MIND BEHIND THE MAGIC

Azlfa Fatima is a rising voice in contemporary fiction, crafting heartfelt stories that echo with emotion, hope, and quiet strength. Her work delicately weaves together themes of friendship, resilience, identity, and the messy beauty of growing up — all told through a lens of rich, lyrical prose.

With a natural gift for storytelling and an eye for life’s small, soul-stirring moments, Azlfa invites readers to feel deeply, dream freely, and believe in second chances. Her debut novel, Thirty Things Map We Made, is not just a story — it’s a map of becoming, healing, and finding your way home.

“A soul-stirring ride full of laughter, wanderlust, and late-night talks. This book made me want to pack a bag and find myself all over again.”

— Riya Malik, author of When We Were Seventeen
